FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


How do potential applicants find the job I posted?

Through a keyword search. The keyword search looks at the job description field that you create to bring up the potential applicant results. It is an exact word search and applicants are advised on how to use the search.

How do I receive resumes?

If you have posted a job, and a job seeker finds your job posting and applies for it, their resume will be sent via email to you. In addition, you will have access to their online profile.

How to do edit my posting?

You can go to the home page where there are view report page where you can edit your form. Just click the edit button and edit which form you want to edit.
